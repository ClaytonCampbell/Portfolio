About

I'm Clayton Campbell, a student of the code and master of the keyboard. I've started my trek onto the path of a coding monk in 2016 and have truly delved into its depths beginning in 2018. I have learned so much, yet as technology advances, so does the effort needed to learn. I'm always ready for any challenge that may be thrown my way.

Some more personal facts about myself is that I'm a musician, being able to sing and play the violin. I also am an avid card game player and tend to enjoy them with friends. I typically strive for competition and wish to reach greater heights in all my accomplishments. I play videogames as well, but that's almost expected in this field.

I'm currently located in Indiana, but that's all up for change in the future. I've stayed around a good portion of the USA to places like Alaska, Seattle, and Texas. I would consider myself well traveled, except for my lack of international locales. I've lived my all my years here in the US but I don't shy from new places.
